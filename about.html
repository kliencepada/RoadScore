Our Mission

RoadScore was created to bridge the communication gap between citizens and local authorities regarding road infrastructure. We provide a simple, interactive platform where anyone can quickly map and rate road conditions, from small potholes to major surface quality issues. Our goal is to drive accountability and facilitate quicker maintenance and improvement projects based on community input.

Key Features

Map-Based Reporting

Use the interactive map to precisely pin the location of a road defect or quality score.

Community Testimonies

Share your stories about local road conditions and how reports have impacted your daily life.

Immediate Feedback

Reports are instantly visible on the map, allowing others to see current conditions in real-time.

Deletion Accountability

Ensure data integrity by requiring a specific reason for the deletion of any submitted report.

Technology Used

This application is built using standard web technologies for maximum compatibility and ease of deployment:

  • HTML5 / CSS3: For structure and styling.
  • Vanilla JavaScript: For all map interactions, data storage, and form handling.
  • Bootstrap 5: For responsive design and UI components (like the navbar and modal).
  • Leaflet.js: The leading open-source library for interactive maps.
  • Local Storage: Reports and testimonies are saved directly in your browser, maintaining persistence across sessions.
